About Us

Born into a family with over half a century of experience in the world of antiques, I grew up surrounded by history, craftsmanship, and stories engraved into timeless pieces. What began as a family tradition soon became a personal passion, collecting relics that spoke to me beyond their form. Over the years, I developed not just an eye for detail but a deep appreciation for the legacy behind every object. This love for preserving the past naturally evolved into Kanz, a space dedicated to celebrating treasures, heritage, and storytelling.

With a background in digital content creation and over 50 million organic views and 250,000+ followers across platforms, I’ve learned the art of bringing stories to life. Through Kanz, I merge my expertise in storytelling with my lifelong fascination for antiquities, creating an online experience unlike any other. Every item curated here carries a tale of cultures, craftsmanship, and journeys across generations waiting to be rediscovered.

At 20, I wanted to change how people, especially the youth, see antiques. While many antique dealers cater to the older audience, Kanz focuses on the youth as well as having something for everyone, offering treasures that speak to modern collectors with timeless taste. Beyond selling, Kanz aspires to create museum like experiences at exhibitions and events, where everyone has the chance not only to admire but to own a piece of history. This is more than a business, it’s a movement to revive appreciation for the past in the hearts of the present.
