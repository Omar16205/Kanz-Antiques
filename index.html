About Us

Kanz started out as an idea. A portal to nostalgia and memories. Built on providing something with a story and history for everyone.

Thanks to its British, Kurdish, and Iraqi origins. It transcends borders, languages, and culture. Our aim is preserve and provide pieces that represent the human condition for the consumer, and to further the cultural enrichment of everywhere we operate

Kanz started out as a hobby and a small project by university students. Born in the heart of Erbil, we aimed to have a presence in any event that would host us. Providing diverse antique items and collectible pieces suited for all tastes. Now we reach above and beyond to have an impact and provide a window to forgotten but beautiful pieces from around the world
